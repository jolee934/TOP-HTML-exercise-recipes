How to make lasanga

Use this recipe to make the best lasagna!

The ingredients you need are:

  • Ricotta cheese
  • Pasta sheet
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Take out the pasta sheet and line them in a baking tray
  2. Add ricotta cheese in between each pasta sheet
  3. Bake for 45 minutes at 450 degrees and sprinkle parmesan on top
